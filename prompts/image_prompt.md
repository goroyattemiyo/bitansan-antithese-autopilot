# 画像生成プロンプト方針

微炭酸アンチテーゼの投稿用画像を生成するためのプロンプトを作成してください。

## 必須要素

- Original 2D anime-style dark idol duo
- Unit name concept: 微炭酸アンチテーゼ, AI music-born dark idol duo
- Netami: purple hair, twin tails, deep purple theme, S emblem, spite / spotlight / selfish motif, provocative expression, slightly sharp tooth, strong jealous energy
- Sonemi: blue hair, twin tails, night-blue theme, M emblem, melancholy / mirror / missing motif, fragile expression, eyes not fully smiling, anxious and dependent mood
- Duo palette: purple, blue, black
- 5-head-tall cute character proportion
- high-quality anime rendering
- dark idol mood, yami-kawaii and toxic-cute but not too horror
- black-based idol outfit with purple accents for Netami and blue accents for Sonemi
- expressive but not horror
- no text, no logo, no watermark, no letters

## 曲・投稿別の補助要素

- 01「いいね、奪ってあげる。」: SNS hearts, notifications, neon, chains, black purple and black blue cyber dark-idol outfits
- 02「センター病棟」: symbolic hospital / ward motif, white black purple and white black blue, no gore
- 03「わたしじゃダメなの？」: Sonemi focused, rainy window, mirror, plush toy, blue-white lace dress, quiet melancholy
- 04「毒蜜シンドローム」: candy gothic, purple, blue, pink, black, hearts, ribbon, glossy poison-honey motif
- 05「共犯者コール」: live stage, microphone, penlights, confetti, dramatic stage lights
- daily posts: backstage, casual outfits, smartphone, ego-searching, waiting for notifications, manager-view composition

## 避ける要素

- real person likeness
- existing anime / manga / game style imitation
- excessive sexualization
- gore
- horror that is too scary
- S/M as sexual meaning
- characters looking too young
- unreadable text

## 出力形式

画像生成用の英語プロンプトのみを出力してください。説明文は不要です。
